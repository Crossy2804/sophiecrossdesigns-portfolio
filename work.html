My Work

Below you will find examples of work I have done from websites to logos. More of my work is featured on my Instagram or Facebook, links to them can be found at the bottom of the page.

Websites

Emma Jayne Photography & Makeup Artistry

This site is one i created for my sister, the focus of the site was her photography. To make sure the photos were the focus the design was simple and very little colour was used. The site is not live but the link will allow you to view the site, a little warning that the site is not responsive, so on mobile devices this site will not look good.


Logos

YeastSpread

I was asked to fix their logo as the sides were cut off and they wanted borders added to make it stand out more. They also asked me to make it into a psychedelic version, one using the red and yellow colours and one using green and purple.


Hopeless Hallucinations

This was a fun logo, using photoshop to create the neon look to it, taking inspiration from 80s neon pictures that we see online. I used blue, purples and pinks as those colours worked well together and they are the colours you see a lot in neon images.
